Regular Curriculum


The Regular curriculum in junior high school is designed to provide a well-rounded education and build a foundation of knowledge and skills in various subject. Here are some common subjects that are typically included:



  • English: the study of the English language, including its grammar, vocabulary, literature, and various forms of communication.
  • Mathematics: the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns, and the relationships between them, using logical reasoning and precise methods of calculation.
  • Science: the systematic study of the natural world, including the observation, experimentation, and explanation of phenomena through the application of evidence-based methods and logical reasoning.
  • Araling Panlipunan: focuses on the study of society, culture, history, geography, economics, and politics, with the aim of developing an understanding of the country's social, political, and economic landscape.
  • Filipino: the study of the Filipino language, its grammar, vocabulary, literature, and cultural aspects, with the goal of promoting effective communication and appreciation of Filipino culture and heritage.
  • MAPEH: an acronym for Music, Arts, Physical Education, and Health, a subject that encompasses the study and practical application of these four areas to promote holistic development, creative expression, physical fitness, and overall well-being among students.
  • TLE: stands for Technology and Livelihood Education, a subject that focuses on equipping students with practical skills and knowledge related to various trades, entrepreneurship, agriculture, home economics, and technology, with the aim of preparing them for employment or self-employment opportunities in the future.


The regular curriculum in Junior High School is designed to provide students with a broad educational foundation, nurturing their academic, physical, artistic, and ethical development. It aims to equip them with essential knowledge, skills, and values to prepare them for Senior High School and beyond.
