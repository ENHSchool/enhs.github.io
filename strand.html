WHAT IS SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL?


The purpose of Senior High School is to prepare you for college by giving the knowledge, abilities, and skills needed to pursue successful professional routes in the future.


For Grade 10 graduates, it means beginning a new academic adventure. It may appear scary at first, especially when deciding which strand to take or which school to attend, but finding the proper track and strand that suits your talents and skills will undoubtedly help relieve your concerns and get you started.


Echague National High School offers the Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) Track. The strands under the Academic Track are Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM). While under TVL, the institution offers Information and Communications Technology specializing in Programming or Information and Communications Technology specializing in Animation.


These various tracks, strands, and specialities are designed to help you identify which paths best suit your abilities, passions, and skills. Selecting the appropriate Senior High School strand can assist motivate you for your future job. When you select a strand that interests you, studying becomes more pleasurable because the strands are built and tailored to meet your interests. Furthermore, it broadens your knowledge and skills while also exposing you to your chosen topic of study in preparation for college.
