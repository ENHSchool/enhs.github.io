What is Junior High School?


Junior High School is the educational stage that follows elementary school and precedes senior high school. It covers Grades 7 to 10. During this stage, students undergo a curriculum that focuses on providing them with a comprehensive education and preparing them for the next level of their academic journey.


ENHS has three Junior High School curriculum available and these are: Special Program in Journalism (SPJ), Science Technology and Engineering (STE), and the Regular Basic Education Curriculum.



Junior High School equips students with the necessary knowledge, competencies, and values needed to thrive academically and prepare for future endeavor.
